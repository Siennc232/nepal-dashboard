Methodology Guidebook

1. Survey Implementation

Data Collection: January-February 2025

Sample Size: 560 completed surveys (95.2% response rate)

Coverage: All 5 wards of Khumbu Pasang Lhamu Rural Municipality

Method: Face-to-face interviews in Nepali and Sherpa languages

2. GNH Framework Adaptation

Based on Bhutan's Gross National Happiness index, adapted for Nepal mountain context:

  • 11 domains (including Tourism as additional domain)
  • 77 questions covering objective and subjective indicators
  • 10-point scale for most responses
  • Domain scores calculated as percentage of maximum possible

3. Domain Score Calculation

Standard Domains (1-9):

  1. Sum all question scores within domain
  2. Divide by maximum possible score
  3. Convert to percentage

Special Calculations:
